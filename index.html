About Me

Pop Quiz: What do you get when you cross a car salesman with a hippie on a three month vacation in California? Meeeeee! Hi, I’m Brandon. I was born on Thanksgiving Day(or Night), 1981, a mere 15 minutes shy of midnight. Fun Fact: I was born with a mild case of jaundice, and to cure it my grandfather used to hold me outside under the sun to soak in those vitamin D rays. It worked! And now here I am, just a few white hairs (because I won’t get any grays) north of 40 years old, married, and back to school—for the 3rd time. But this one’s gonna stick! In case you’re wondering what I did in school the other 2 times, I’ll tell you… …when I get to remake this website after I’ve learned how to make it better. Until then, Cheers!
